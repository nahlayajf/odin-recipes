Turkey Sandwich Recipe

Description

This classic, high-quality turkey sandwich recipe features toasted bread, savory bacon, crisp vegetables, and a flavorful spread to elevate a standard lunch into a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices whole wheat or sourdough bread, toasted
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 6 oz deli-sliced roasted turkey breast
  • 2-4 lettuce leaves (romaine or iceberg)
  • 4-6 thin slices tomato

Instructions

  1. Prep the Bread: Lightly toast the bread until golden brown. This keeps the sandwich sturdy.
  2. Spread Mayo: Spread mayonnaise generously on one side of each slice of toast.
  3. Layer the Ingredients: On two slices of the toast (mayo side up), layer the turkey,tomato slices, and lettuce. Season the tomato slices with a little salt and pepper for better flavor.
  4. Assemble: Top with the remaining two slices of toast (mayo side down).
